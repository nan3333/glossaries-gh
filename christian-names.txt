    The name Nathan has ties to both Nathaniel
    and Jonathan, with all three names meaning
    something similar to “Gift of God” or “God
    has given.” In the Old Testament, Nathan
    was a prophet and one of King David's
    sons; he acted as a messenger to King
    David and is considered one of Jesus'
    ancestors.
